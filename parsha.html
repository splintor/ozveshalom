Oz VeShalom - Netivot Shalom
Hebrew Daf Parshat HaShavua

The weekly Torah portion flyer (in Hebrew) includes a commentary explaining how that week's portion teaches us the importance of peace and what we must do to pursue it. It shows how the values of peace, human dignity, justice, and freedom are found in the familiar words of our tradition that we read every week in synagogue.

This week's Parsha:
םירבד תשרפ
See bottom of page for past issues.

The flyer is distributed every Shabbat in over 300 synagogues around Israel. If your synagogue doesn't receive our flyer, and you would like to distribute it, please email us with your name, address, phone number, and the number of copies you would like to receive.
