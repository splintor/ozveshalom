Articles & Position Papers

The following is a Directory of articles dealing with the peace process in the Middle East, Judaism, Zionism and Israel as a jewish & democratic state. The articles describe our position about the just way towards peace,current events in Israel and the Jewish world, jewish thought & philosophy, and ideas that can help support the Peace process in the region. You can search our article database (at the present search is only available in English), or check out our specific peace and judaism directories (note that some of the articles are listed under more than one category).

Our weekly parsha commentary also teaches us the importance of peace, as found in the familiar words of our tradition (in Hebrew).
